OUR MISSION

Help solving social and environmental issues using data science and technology.

OUR GOALS

To help any social or environmental organization to harness their data:

  • by giving meaning to the data they have,
  • by guiding them to their next action through prediction for example,
  • by building tools to make their work easier, or more effective.


To be a space where data scientists and developers can collaborate on meaningful projects with a positive and tangible impact on the world.

 

​To share our knowledge and train more developers, so that our impact is expanded.

THE TEAM

Data For Good is community-run!

We are software developers, data scientists and analysts, but also project coordinators with no required programming experience, all connected by our desire to use data to improve the world.
We help each other track down datasets, refine models, improve visualizations, team up on apps, debug code, promote work, and connect with communities who need our analysis.

In the footsteps of similar organizations, we are working to develop a cohesive structure to better support volunteers in learning, working, and leading projects while remaining committed to our mission of producing data projects that have a positive and tangible impact on the world.
